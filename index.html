Aspera, Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering

Aspera, the Entrepreneurship-Cell (also known as Startup Club) of Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering was set up to encourage entrepreneurship among students. It is a student driven community backed by the college management.

Aspera is connected to the National Entrepreneurship Network under the Wadhwani Foundation. Wadhwani Foundation is a non-profit organisation which inspires, educates and supports early-stage entrepreneurs.

Anyone can become a Startup Club member.Startup Club members participate in different sets of programs organized through the Startup Club (100+ participating members).
